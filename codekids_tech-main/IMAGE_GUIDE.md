## Image Requirements
- **Format:** JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Recommended Size:** At least 800x800 pixels for best quality
- **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1) or close to square for team members
- **File Size:** Optimize for web (under 500KB each recommended)
